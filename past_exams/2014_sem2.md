### a) Convert each sentence to CNF
```
~(P ^ ~Q) v ~(~S ^ ~T)  = ((~P v Q) v (S v T))
                        = ~P v Q v S v T
~(T v Q)                = ~T ^ ~Q
U -> (~T -> (~S ^ P))   = U -> (~(~S ^ P) -> T)
                        = U -> ((S v ~P) -> T)
                        = U -> (~(S v ~P) v T)
                        = U -> (~S ^ ~P v T)
                        = ~U v (~S ^ ~P v T)
                        = (~U v ~S) ^ (~U v ~P v T)

1) ~P v Q v S v T
2) ~T
3) ~Q
4) ~U v ~S
5) ~U v ~P v T
```
